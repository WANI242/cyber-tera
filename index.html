Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services. Need more help? Contact us directly!

What are the main benefits of installing a CCTV system?

CCTV systems offer enhanced security by deterring crime, providing surveillance for evidence collection, monitoring property and staff, and giving you remote access to live footage for peace of mind, even when you're away from your premises in Jinja.

What is the difference between a wired and wireless network?

Wired networks use physical cables (Ethernet) for connectivity, offering stability and speed, while wireless networks (Wi-Fi) provide flexibility and mobility without cables.

Can you upgrade my old wiring?

Absolutely! We offer rewiring and upgrades to ensure your electrical system is safe and up to code.

How do Access Control Systems work, and why do I need one?

Access Control Systems manage who can enter and exit specific areas using methods like key cards, biometrics (fingerprint), or keypads. They are essential for restricting unauthorized access, tracking entry/exit times, enhancing safety for employees or residents, and protecting valuable assets in commercial or residential settings.

Do you offer network maintenance and support?

Yes, we provide ongoing maintenance, troubleshooting, and support to ensure your network remains reliable and secure.

Do you provide emergency electrical repairs?

Yes, we offer emergency repair services for urgent electrical issues.
