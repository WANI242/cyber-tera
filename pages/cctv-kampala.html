CCTV that fits Kampala's realities

Kampala traffic, intermittent power, and the sheer density of premises mean CCTV systems here need to be planned carefully. Our installers design systems that keep running through load-shedding with inline UPS units, resist storm surges with proper surge protection, and use local-storage NVRs so you are not dependent on unreliable uplink speeds for playback.

We regularly work across Nakasero, Kololo, Bugolobi, Ntinda, Bukoto, Naalya, Muyenga, Kansanga, Kisaasi, and the Industrial Area. For commercial properties in Nakawa and along Jinja Road we deliver multi-camera IP systems with centralized recording, covert camera placement, and remote administration so site managers can check in from anywhere.

Typical Kampala installation packages

  • Home package: 4–6 HD cameras at entrances + perimeter, NVR with 1TB storage, mobile app, UPS backup
  • Shop/office package: 6–12 IP cameras (till-area + entrance + stockroom), 2TB NVR, remote alert rules, 24/7 motion recording
  • Warehouse/industrial: PTZ perimeter + fixed interior cameras, multiple NVRs, network segmentation, optional access-control integration
