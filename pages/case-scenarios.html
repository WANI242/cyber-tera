Typical project plans

These are representative plans based on common client needs. We tailor the final design after a site survey.

Retail · Jinja

Retail CCTV rollout for multiple shop areas

Need
Clear coverage of entrances, tills, stock areas, and customer movement without creating a system that is hard to review.
Plan
Place fixed cameras at high-value points, keep the recorder protected, add UPS backup, and configure remote viewing for the owner or manager.
Handover
Train staff on playback, exporting clips, mobile access, and what to check weekly so the system remains useful after installation.
Business · Kampala

Office network refresh for a growing team

Need
Stable video calls, better Wi-Fi coverage, separate guest access, and network equipment that stays online during short outages.
Plan
Survey desk locations, cable paths, and signal weak points, then combine structured cabling, managed switching, access points, and a cabinet UPS.
Handover
Document network names, router access, device locations, and basic troubleshooting steps for the person responsible on site.
Industrial · Mukono

Warehouse electrical upgrade and safety review

Need
Reduce nuisance tripping, improve circuit safety, and understand whether old wiring can support current equipment loads.
Plan
Inspect distribution boards, earthing, cable condition, and load balance, then recommend repairs, protection devices, or staged rewiring.
Handover
Provide a clear scope of completed work, circuit notes, and next maintenance priorities so the owner can plan safely.
