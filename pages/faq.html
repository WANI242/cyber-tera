Q1: What are the main benefits of installing a CCTV system?

A1: CCTV systems offer enhanced security by deterring crime, providing surveillance for evidence collection, monitoring property and staff, and giving you remote access to live footage for peace of mind, even when you're away from your premises in Jinja.

Q2: How do Access Control Systems work, and why do I need one?

A2: Access Control Systems manage who can enter and exit specific areas using methods like key cards, biometrics (fingerprint or facial recognition), or keypads. They are essential for restricting unauthorized access, tracking entry/exit times, enhancing safety for employees or residents, and protecting valuable assets in commercial or residential settings.

Q3: What is the difference between a wired and wireless network?

A3: Wired networks use physical cables (Ethernet) for connectivity, offering stability and speed, while wireless networks (Wi-Fi) provide flexibility and mobility without cables.

Q4: Can you help set up Wi-Fi coverage for a large building?

A4: Yes, we design and install Wi-Fi solutions for homes, offices, and large buildings, ensuring strong and reliable coverage throughout your premises.

Q5: What is a VPN and do I need one for my business?

A5: A VPN (Virtual Private Network) creates a secure connection over the internet, protecting your data and allowing remote access to your network. It's highly recommended for businesses with remote workers or sensitive data.

Q6: How can I improve my network speed?

A6: Upgrading your hardware, optimizing your network configuration, and ensuring proper cabling can significantly improve your network speed. We can assess and recommend the best solutions for your needs.

A7: Network security protects your data and devices from unauthorized access, cyber threats, and attacks. We implement firewalls, strong passwords, and monitoring to keep your network safe.
