Kampala electrical work, done properly

Kampala's electrical infrastructure is a mix of old colonial-era wiring in inner suburbs and modern installations in new estates — and a lot of "patch jobs" in between. Our approach is to assess first, then propose a plan: we will tell you honestly whether a circuit needs a simple repair, a partial rewire, or a full distribution-board upgrade.

Typical jobs we handle across Kampala include residential rewiring in Bugolobi, Kololo, and Naguru; distribution-board upgrades in Ntinda and Muyenga; commercial fit-outs along Kampala Road, Kololo Avenue, and in the Industrial Area; and standby generator wiring with automatic transfer switches for small businesses that can't afford downtime.

What we do in Kampala

  • Complete house rewiring (new build & retrofit)
  • Distribution board (DB) replacement and upgrades
  • Fault-finding and emergency repairs
  • LED lighting design and installation
  • Standby generator & ATS wiring
  • UPS, inverter, and solar-hybrid installations
  • Surge protection and earthing system upgrades
  • Commercial electrical fit-outs (offices, shops, restaurants)

Licensed, insured, documented

Every installation comes with a written scope, an itemised quote, and a completion certificate documenting circuits, ratings, and test results — useful for insurance, landlords, and property sales. We work to ERA (Electricity Regulatory Authority) wiring regulations and only use approved cable and accessories.

Kampala areas we serve

Central Division, Nakawa, Makindye, Kawempe, Rubaga, plus Wakiso (Kira, Namugongo, Bweyogerere, Kyaliwajjala, Kyanja). Ask for a visit — most free site visits within Kampala are scheduled within 48 hours.
