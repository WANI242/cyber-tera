Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

CYBER TERA UGANDA LIMITED ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect when you use this website or request a quote, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide on the Get a Quote form: name, email, phone number, location, and project description.
  • Your preferred contact method.
  • Basic technical information automatically collected by our hosting provider (e.g. IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and analytics.

How we use it

  • To respond to your quote request and follow up on the services you asked about.
  • To schedule site surveys, installations, or maintenance visits.
  • To improve our website and services.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your information. We share it only with service providers that help us operate the website and deliver our services (for example, our email delivery provider, EmailJS, which transports quote-request submissions to our inbox).

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you by emailing cyberteraug@gmail.com.
